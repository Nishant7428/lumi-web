Our AI Agent autonomously resolves up to 75% of non-emergency calls, freeing your call takers to focus on more critical emergencies. Call Routing & Escalation
Seamlessly integrate AI into your existing operations. Whether PSTN or SIP, we can integrate with your current Call Handling System (CHS) to ensure smooth handling of non-emergency calls with intelligent routing, instant escalation capabilities, and automated follow-up communications.

Optimize operations with real-time analytics and automated QA. Track call deflection rates, response times, and call types through a comprehensive dashboard. Maintain complete call records with automated transcription, categorization, and performance tracking that meet your retention requirements.
